# 1.3 Motivation

The stark realization regarding AI hallucinations has sparked a major pivot in how we approach this field. Rather than throwing out rules entirely or relying completely on black-box AI, there is a strong motivation to explore hybrid approaches. 

Our motivation for this project stems from a desire to build systems that actually understand the blueprint of the database they are querying. We are driven to create a highly transparent, schema-aware pipeline designed specifically to convert natural language into SQL without the risks of hallucination. Instead of blindly trusting a model to generate code, our approach breaks the process down into logical steps. 

Finally, we are deeply motivated to tackle what is arguably the biggest hurdle in deploying AI tools: user trust. If a user doesn't understand how a system got its answer, they won't trust it. This drove us to develop an interactive visualization frontend that literally breaks down the "thought process" of our NLP engine, showing the user exactly how their everyday sentence was mapped before any SQL is executed.
